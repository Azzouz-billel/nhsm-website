About the school

The National Higher School of Mathematics.

NHSM is a grande école in Algiers dedicated to pure and applied mathematics. Students spend their first years on a shared mathematical core, then specialise in an advanced track. This page is a quick introduction for newcomers — the fields, the people, and life on campus.
